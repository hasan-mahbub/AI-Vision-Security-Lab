Artificial Intelligence and Cybersecurity Research

Welcome to AI Vision & Security Lab Homepage!

At the AI Vision & Security Lab of New Mexico Tech, we are dedicated to advancing the field of artificial intelligence with a particular focus on computer vision and machine learning. Our research endeavors aim to harness the transformative power of AI to tackle complex security challenges. By bridging the gap between theoretical innovation and practical application, we strive to develop robust solutions that enhance security measures and contribute to the safer digital infrastructure. Join us as we explore the depths of AI and unlock potentials for a secure tomorrow.

Key Research Areas

  • Machine Learning/ Deep Learning
  • AI in Cyber Security
  • Security of Autonomous Vehicles
  • Intelligent Sound Synthesis
  • Human Factor based Behavioral Abstraction and Modeling
  • Insider Threat Analysis and Detection
